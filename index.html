Tiger Woods is an icon in the game of golf. He is one of those once in a generation type players who changed the game for the better. He has one 14 major championships and over 106 professional wins.

Tiger has two children named Sam and Charlie. He now shares custody of those two with his ex-wife Elin Nordegren. They divorced after a big scandal in November of 2009.
